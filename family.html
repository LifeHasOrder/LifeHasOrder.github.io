Family & Friends Provide Inspiration & Meaning



“Friends share all things.”― Pythagoras

I was fortunate to find my best friend early in life. I even convinced her to marry me, and now I have someone with whom I can share all things.



Despite being an introvert, I find that life is much brighter when I can share my experiences with someone I love. I have spent my life surrounded by an array of great people, and I am grateful for the contributions they made towards my development as a person and as a scientist. Thanks to my family & friends for providing love and support, to my educators for pushing me to always go further, and to my wife for accompanying me on this great journey.
